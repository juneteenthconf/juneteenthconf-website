Streaming to you wherever you are!

The Mission of JuneteenthConf is to provide a judgement free space for underrepresented people in tech to learn and grow and to amplify their voices. To be a counterbalance to the biases, implicit or otherwise, that hold our industry and our society back.

JuneteenthConf is a free virtual tech conference made for and featuring under-represented people in Technology. We are in the process of planning and need your help. Speakers, Event Organizers, Developers, and Designers, we can't do this without you. Join the effort and let's make an amazing event to celebrate and empower people through technology.
